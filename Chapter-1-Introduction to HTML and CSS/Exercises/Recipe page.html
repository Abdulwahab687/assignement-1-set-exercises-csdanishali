Maple Glazed Salmon

This elegant yet simple maple glazed salmon combines the rich flavors of pure maple syrup with soy sauce and garlic to create a sweet and savory glaze that caramelizes beautifully when baked. Perfect for a weeknight dinner but impressive enough for guests, this dish pairs wonderfully with steamed vegetables and rice.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together maple syrup, soy sauce, minced garlic, black pepper, olive oil, lemon juice, and red pepper flakes (if using) until well combined.
  3. Place the salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet, skin side down.
  4. Brush the maple mixture generously over each salmon fillet, reserving some for basting later.
  5. Let the salmon marinate for 15 minutes at room temperature.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-18 minutes, basting with the remaining glaze halfway through cooking time.
  7. The salmon is done when it flakes easily with a fork and reaches an internal temperature of 145째F (63째C).
  8. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley before serving.
  9. Serve immediately with your choice of sides such as steamed asparagus and fluffy rice.
