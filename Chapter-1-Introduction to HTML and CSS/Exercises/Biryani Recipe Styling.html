Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

These are seriously the BEST chocolate chip cookies I've ever made! They're super soft in the middle but have those amazing crispy edges. I got this recipe from my grandma and tweaked it a bit over the years. Trust me, they disappear really fast whenever I make them for friends!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar (packed down)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (I like semi-sweet but use whatever you prefer!)
  • 1 cup walnuts - chopped (totally optional)

Method

  1. Heat oven to 375Â°F
  2. Mix the flour, baking soda & salt in a bowl
  3. In another bowl, beat the butter and both sugars until it looks creamy
  4. Beat in eggs & vanilla
  5. Slowly add in the flour mixture - don't overmix!
  6. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto cookie sheets (leave some room between them)
  8. Bake 9-11 min until the edges look golden brown
  9. Let them cool for a couple minutes before moving to a rack
  10. Try not to eat them all at once! (I always fail at this step lol)
