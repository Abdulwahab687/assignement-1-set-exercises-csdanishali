Overview

Gladiator II is the long-awaited sequel to Ridley Scott's 2000 Oscar-winning epic, "Gladiator." Set 20 years after the events of the original film, this new chapter in the Roman saga follows Lucius Verus, the grown-up son of Lucilla and nephew of the tyrannical emperor Commodus from the first film.

The film features breathtaking battle sequences, intricate political intrigue, and explores themes of revenge, honor, and the corrupting nature of power—all hallmarks of the original masterpiece. Directed by Ridley Scott and featuring a star-studded cast, Gladiator II promises to be one of the most anticipated historical epics of 2023.

This sequel continues the legacy of one of cinema's most beloved historical epics, returning viewers to the glory and brutality of ancient Rome. With stunning visuals, epic battle choreography, and a deeply personal story of revenge and redemption, Gladiator II aims to capture the spirit of the original while blazing its own path.
