America's Most Beautiful National Parks

Yosemite National Park

Yosemite Valley is known for its breathtaking views, towering granite cliffs, and magnificent waterfalls. This iconic view features El Capitan on the left and Bridalveil Fall on the right.

Grand Canyon National Park

The Grand Canyon is a steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River in Arizona. This stunning view from the South Rim shows the incredible layers of rock and dramatic colors during sunset.

Yellowstone National Park

Yellowstone is famous for its geothermal features, including the Grand Prismatic Spring shown here. The vibrant colors are created by different species of heat-loving bacteria living around the edges of the mineral-rich water.
